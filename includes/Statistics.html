Statistics about car accidents in israel

Fatalities: In 2022, there were 351 road deaths in Israel [ITF]. This translates to a road mortality rate of 3.6 deaths per 100,000 people [ITF].

Accidents: There were 56,948 road accidents involving casualties in 2022 [National Road Safety Authority].

Severity: While fatalities decreased slightly in 2022 compared to 2021, the number of people seriously injured rose [The Media Line].

Pedestrians: It's important to note that pedestrians made up 31% of all road deaths in 2022 [ITF].

Vehicle Type: Motorcycles pose a higher risk, with the number of serious injuries or fatalities in motorcycle accidents being 17.8 times higher than private vehicles [National Road Safety Authority].
